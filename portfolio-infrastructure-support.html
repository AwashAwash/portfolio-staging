High-Availability IT Infrastructure Across Multiple Data Centers

This project focused on providing continuous operational support for large-scale enterprise IT infrastructure deployed across two geographically separated data centers. The primary objective was to maintain 99.9% system uptime while ensuring optimal performance, resilience, and seamless disaster recovery readiness.

Key Responsibilities

  • Provided ongoing operational support for colocated servers across multiple data centers
  • Ensured system availability, performance tuning, and proactive fault resolution
  • Managed physical relocation of enterprise hardware with minimal downtime
  • Rack mounting, structured cabling, labeling, and power optimization
  • Reconfigured servers and network devices following relocation

Infrastructure & Vendor Platforms

HP Enterprise Servers, IBM Systems, Oracle PCA Racks, Structured Cabling Standards, Rack & Power Distribution Units (PDUs), Enterprise Storage & Compute Infrastructure

Networking & Connectivity Enhancements

  • Upgraded core network routing and firewall infrastructure
  • Implemented high-speed optical inter-DC connectivity
  • Configured FC-LC optical cables and SFP/SFP+ modules
  • Optimized traffic flow between Primary and DR sites

Impact & Results

Successfully reduced inter-data-center latency by 20%, improved failover reliability between DR and primary sites, and strengthened overall business continuity capabilities through resilient infrastructure design and execution.
