Enterprise Infrastructure Engineering & Operations

Designing, deploying, and operating secure, highly available infrastructure across data centers and cloud platforms.

Project Snapshot

  • Role: Infrastructure Engineer
  • Scope: Data Center, Cloud & DR
  • Focus: Availability, Security, Automation
  • Environment: Enterprise / Government / Telecom

Overview

As an Infrastructure Engineer, I was responsible for building and operating resilient IT environments that supported mission-critical services. The role required hands-on involvement from physical infrastructure to virtualization, networking, security, and disaster recovery planning.

Key Contributions

  • Designed and maintained highly available on-prem and cloud infrastructure.
  • Managed enterprise Linux and Windows Server environments.
  • Implemented disaster recovery and business continuity strategies.
  • Performed hardware lifecycle management, rack deployments, and cabling.
  • Strengthened infrastructure security through hardening and monitoring.
  • Collaborated with vendors and cross-functional teams for infrastructure upgrades.

Technologies & Platforms

Linux (RHEL, CentOS, Ubuntu), Windows Server, VMware vSphere / ESXi, Docker, Kubernetes, AWS (EC2, S3, RDS), SAN/NAS Storage, Firewalls, VLANs, VPNs, Monitoring (Grafana, Prometheus), Backup & Replication, Automation (Bash, Terraform, Ansible)

Outcome & Impact

Delivered stable, scalable, and secure infrastructure with 99.9% uptime, reduced operational risks, and improved recovery readiness for enterprise workloads.
